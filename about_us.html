A message from The President of BATC

It is a great honor for me to serve as the President of the Bangladesh-American Tech Community (BATC). Our vision is simple yet powerful: to connect innovation, culture, and community by bringing together talented professionals of Bangladeshi origin who are shaping the global tech landscape.

Through BATC, we aim to create a platform where ideas are shared, collaborations are built, and opportunities are unlocked. Whether it is fostering career growth, celebrating our heritage, or building meaningful networks, BATC stands as a bridge between our culture and the future of technology.

— Mizanur Choudhury
President, BATC

Our Vision

To become the most trusted learning platform in Bangladesh — and eventually, South Asia — helping people move from education to execution, learning to earning. We want to see a future where thousands of students from villages, towns, and cities alike are building careers they love — freelancing, working at top companies, or launching their own ventures.

Our Mission

To empower a generation of learners with practical, affordable, and transformative education — built for Bangladesh, ready for the world. At Enthrall IT, our mission is rooted in a simple but bold idea: Education should change lives — not just look good on paper. We’re here to break the traditional learning model.

No more overpriced courses, outdated theories, or one-size-fits-all solutions. Instead, we focus on hands-on, real-world skills that open doors — to jobs, freelancing, entrepreneurship, and global opportunities. Our goal is to support the underdogs — the student from a small town, the job-seeker with a dream, the curious soul who just wants a better life. We don’t care where you start. We care where you’re going.
